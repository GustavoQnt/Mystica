# Mystica UI Design

**Date:** 2026-03-12

**Direction:** Luxe Mystical, contemplative and minimal.

## Visual System

- Deep plum and blue-black background with soft golden accents used sparingly.
- Serif display typography for titles, card names, and sacred highlights.
- Light geometric sans-serif for body copy and long-form reading text.
- Generous negative space and centered compositions.

## Motion

- Slow fade and rise for section entrances.
- Card fan should feel suspended over velvet with eased motion and soft inertia.
- Card reveal should be the strongest motion moment, but still restrained.
- Streaming text should appear progressively with soft opacity changes.

## Layout

- `/`: personal sanctuary with today's advice as the hero and a compact recent history section.
- `/reading`: intention form and spread picker, then the animated fan.
- `/reading/[id]`: card reveal + streaming interpretation + practical advice.
- `/history`: calm archive of completed readings.

## Interaction Rules

- Gold is reserved for sacred emphasis: main CTA, revealed card title, key labels.
- Reading flow remains linear and focused; no dashboard clutter.
- If card images are missing, reveal cards still render elegantly with a text-first fallback.
